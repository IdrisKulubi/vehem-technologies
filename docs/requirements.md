## 3. Legal & Compliance Requirements

- **Privacy Policy:**  
  Required by Apple to ensure you have a plan for handling user data.
- **Terms of Service:**  
  Defines the rules for using your website and services.
- **Copyright Footer:**  
  Must include a `© 2026 Vehem Technologies Limited` notice.

## 4. Technical Standards (2026)

- **Mobile-First Design:**  
  At least 73% of users leave non-responsive sites; ensure full compatibility with iPhone/iPad for Apple's reviewers.
- **Page Speed:**  
  Your Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) should be under 2.5 seconds to build trust and authority.
- **No Placeholders:**  
  Avoid _"Coming Soon"_ banners or registrar placeholders, as these lead to immediate rejection by Apple.
